My Appreciation for Mozart

I grew up listening to bollywood songs. But when I was in my late teenage years, I started learning guitar. I learnt guitar through Youtube videos. One day while I was watching some guitar playing videos on youtube, I was recommended Turkish March by Mozart. I listened to it, I felt like that it is taking me to a different journey of life. Then I started listening to Mozart Music on a daily basis. His compositions are not like millionare motivation, breakup, sad death but its more like real life. His music gives me mixture of feelings like rememberence of past, opening of new dimensions of life, something you've never thought. It makes me numb, sometimes and also suspicious.
